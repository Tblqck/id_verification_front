About

Identity you can build on.

idntory exists because verifying who someone is shouldn't require a week of vendor integration work or a compliance team on standby. We build the identity layer so product teams can focus on the product.

Why we started

Trust shouldn't be a bottleneck.

Every product that touches money, access or reputation eventually needs to know who's on the other end of the connection. Most teams solve this by duct-taping together a document scanner, a face-match SDK and a spreadsheet of sanctions lists. We think that's the wrong default — verification should be infrastructure, not a project.

01

One API, not five vendors

Document checks, biometric liveness and KYC/AML screening behind a single integration, instead of stitching together point solutions.

02

Honest about where we are

We're a small team building fast. We'd rather tell you exactly what's live and what's on the roadmap than round up — see Compliance for the specifics.

03

Built for the teams that need it most

Fintech, marketplaces, mobility and healthcare teams operate under real regulatory pressure. We design around that reality, not around a demo.
