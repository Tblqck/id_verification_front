Privacy Policy

Last updated 9 July 2026. This policy explains what personal data idntory, Inc. ("idntory", "we", "us") collects through our website, dashboard and verification API, and how we use, store and protect it.

1. What we collect

Depending on how you use idntory, we may process:

  • Account & contact data — name, work email, company, and messages you send us through the site or dashboard.
  • Verification data — identity document images, selfies or liveness frames, and the MRZ/biometric data extracted from them, submitted by our customers' end users through the API.
  • Usage data — API request logs, dashboard activity and basic device/browser information, used for security and debugging.

2. How we use it

We use personal data to operate the verification service (running document, biometric and KYC/AML checks and returning a verdict), to respond to inquiries, to secure our systems against fraud and abuse, and to meet legal and regulatory obligations. We do not sell personal data.

3. Who sees it

Verification data submitted through the API belongs to the customer running the check — we process it on their behalf as described in our Data Processing Addendum. Access within idntory is limited to the systems and personnel that need it to deliver the service, through the permissioned admin dashboard described in our Compliance page. We use a small number of infrastructure and email-delivery subprocessors to run the service; we do not share personal data beyond what's needed to operate it.

4. Retention

Verification data (documents, selfies and liveness frames) is retained according to the retention window configured by the customer running the check, as described in our Compliance page. Contact-form submissions are kept only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have the right to access, correct, or request erasure of your personal data. If you were verified through idntory by one of our customers, the fastest path is usually to contact that business directly, since they control the data; you're also welcome to contact us directly and we'll route the request appropriately.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled? Email info@idntory.com or use our contact form.

This policy may be updated as idntory's product and legal review evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.
