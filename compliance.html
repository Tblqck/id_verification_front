Trust & compliance

Built for regulated industries.

Compliance isn't a bolt-on for us — it shapes how verification data moves, where it's stored and who can see it. Here's exactly where we stand today, and what's still on the roadmap.

Live today

What's already in place.

01

GDPR-aligned handling

Verification data is processed and stored with EU data-protection practices in mind, with erasure supported on request.

02

KYC / AML screening

Sanctions and PEP screening runs as part of the standard verification flow — no separate integration required.

03

Document integrity checks

MRZ cross-validation and tamper detection flag inconsistencies between a document's printed and machine-readable data.

04

Biometric liveness

Active liveness challenges reduce spoofing risk from photos, screens and pre-recorded video during face match.

05

Access-controlled admin tooling

Review and audit verification results through a permissioned admin dashboard, not raw file exports.

06

Configurable data retention

Retention windows for captured documents and biometric frames are configurable per deployment.
